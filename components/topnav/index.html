Top Nav v1.1.0+ HelixUI v1.1.0 or later required

Top Nav is the top navigation menu bar across all breakpoints within the application frame of a HelixUI interface. Use Top Nav to provide access to globally available features, such as account settings, billing, tickets, user management and so on.
